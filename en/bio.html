Early Days 2005-2011

STEREAL was founded in 2005 by Franki (guitar and vocals), Iker (bass guitar), Iñaki (lead guitar) and Billy (drums).

Unpretentious and with high hopes they began to compose songs in styles of pop, rock and folk.

Followers of the DIY (do it yourself) philosophy, they managed to run their own home studio (named by the band “Milcaras”) in Nocedal (Ortuella, Bizkaia) a place to rehearsal and where they recorded 8 songs for their first demo “The Demos Vol.I " published in the digital platform Jamendo in the spring of 2009.

During these years they played countless gigs in bars, parties, local televisions, and competitions, among which include his third place in the competition for new bands of Portugalete in 2009.

From left to right: Iñaki, Franki, Billy & Iker.

Modern Times 2011-2015

Due to the change of residence of Iker (founding member of the band), STEREAL going to become a trio.

At this point begins the composition and subsequent recording of their first LP "IT'S COMING", a 100% STEREAL album, where the band members themselves are responsible for recording, mixing, mastering, graphic design, publishing and distribution. This work will be published during summer of 2015
