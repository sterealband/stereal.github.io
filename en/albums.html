STEREAL "IT'S COMING"

The awaited debut of STEREAL, called ‘IT´S COMING’, is a 12-track album with a total running time of 50 minutes that combines rock, pop and folk music from the 1960s until today.

It has been released through the digital platform Bandcamp , and there is also available a very limited run of 250 vinyl records (including CD) that you can order here

This wonderful limited edition shall consist of:

  • 1 Vinyl record (black color, 140 gr.)
  • 1 Compact Disc, full color printed
  • 1 Poster sleeve cover (cover opens into a 50 X 70cm. poster)
  • 1 Booklet with 16 pages (din-A4 format), including lyrics, pictures…
  • 1 Card with a code to download the digital edition of the album
(Atention: 50 copies have an alternative cover)

The album was recorded, mixed and mastered by the lead singer Franki at “MILCARAS STUDIOS”, the band´s home-studios in Nocedal and Santurce (Bizkaia).The cover art was created by no other than Iñaki, the lead guitar, who has placed a stunning illustration over a picture made by the band´s drummer, Billy, so this is a 100% self-produced album by STEREAL and without any record label behind.

The full tracklisting is:

  1. MOTHER
  2. AMERICA
  3. FRATERNITY
  4. GLORY DAYS
  5. DOWN
  6. TONIGHT
  7. BLACK EYED PEACE
  8. BOSTON
  9. SOMETHING ABOUT YOU
  10. IN THE MIDDLE
  11. ONE FOR ME
  12. ALL MY LIFE
